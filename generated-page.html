Common Questions

Do you offer WhatsApp support?

Yes. Our team is available on WhatsApp for appointment enquiries, basic questions, and follow-up support during clinic hours.

Where is the clinic located?

Soma Plastic Surgery (Clinical Aesthetics) is located in Subang Jaya, Malaysia. Full directions and location details are available on our contact page.

Is parking available at the clinic?

Yes. Convenient parking is available for patients visiting the clinic.

Do I need a referral to book an appointment?

No referral is needed. You can book a consultation directly with our clinic.

Do you accept walk-in visits?

Consultations are by appointment to ensure privacy and adequate time for each patient. We recommend booking in advance.

Does the clinic guarantee results?

Every treatment is planned carefully, but results can vary between individuals. We focus on safe, realistic, and natural outcomes rather than promises.

Will I feel pressured to proceed with treatment?

No. Consultations are for guidance and information. You decide if and when you wish to proceed.

Is my consultation private and confidential?

Yes. All consultations and patient information are handled with strict confidentiality and discretion.

Who will I be consulting with?

All consultations and procedures are personally handled by Dr Soma, a Consultant Plastic and Reconstructive Surgeon.

Can I contact the clinic before deciding?

Absolutely. You are welcome to contact us by phone or WhatsApp to ask general questions before booking a consultation.

Ready when you are

If you are thinking about a change, start with a private conversation. We are here to help you feel clear, calm, and confident in your decision.
